Install MetaMask Wallet

Why is MetaMask needed!?

With MetaMask you can interract with the Ethereum network. Moreover, the benefit of MetaMask is that YOU are storing your private keys instead of letting us to hold to your keys.

Your MetaMask is locked!

Open MetaMask and follow the instructions to unlock it, reload the page and try again.

Switch your MetaMask to MainNet!

Open your wallet, switch your wallet to Main Ethereum Network and reload the page.
