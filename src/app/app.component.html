What do I need to use CanYa?

Here’s what you need to get started:

  • A computer or laptop running the desktop version of Chrome.
  • MetaMask, a digital wallet used specifically with web apps ( Install MetaMask ).
  • CanYaCoins, a form of digital payment that powers CanYa.
Installing MetaMask

To use CanYa, you will need to install MetaMask, a digital wallet. You will need to put money in it to make your first purchase.

Note: A digital wallet like MetaMask acts like a bank account—treat it with respect and make sure you don’t forget your password or the seed words.

Why is MetaMask locked?

Occasionally the page displays a lock screen. This happens because MetaMask automatically locks your account after a certain period of time. To unlock simply click on the MetaMask extension and type in your password.

Getting CanYaCoins

You will need to purchase CAN from an exchange, and then transfer the CAN from your exchange wallet to your MetaMask Wallet. Unfortunately, you cannot use CanYa with only an exchange account.
